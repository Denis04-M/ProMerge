About Us

Our Journey

Founded in 2025 as a senior student project, ProMerge was built with a vision: to transform document management through AI-driven innovation. Recognizing the challenges businesses face with traditional document handling, we developed a solution that seamlessly integrates artificial intelligence with a user-friendly design, making document management smarter and more efficient.

Our Values

Innovation – We continuously integrate the latest technology to enhance user experience and stay ahead in document management.

User-Centric Design – Our solutions are designed with ease of use and efficiency at their core, ensuring a seamless experience.

Integrity – We uphold transparency and trust in every interaction, fostering reliability and ethical responsibility.

Our Mission

Empowering businesses with an intuitive, secure, and AI-driven platform that enhances productivity, safeguards data, and streamlines document management with ease.
